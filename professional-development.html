My Professional Development

This inquiry has significantly contributed to my growth as a teacher. It helped me understand that digital tools should not be used only for engagement, but should support conceptual understanding through effective teaching strategies.

Through this experience, I realised that participation does not always mean understanding. Earlier, I focused more on task completion, but now I understand the importance of explanation, reasoning, and application-based learning.

This inquiry also strengthened my understanding of formative assessment and the importance of identifying misconceptions through questioning and discussion. I learnt that digital tools such as simulations and interactive activities are most effective when combined with meaningful pedagogy.

Going forward, I aim to use digital tools more purposefully in both online and classroom teaching to support deeper thinking and conceptual understanding. Overall, this experience has helped me become a more reflective teacher who focuses not only on engagement, but also on meaningful learning and critical thinking.
