Academic Background

I am a new teacher who is very passionate about helping students easily understand the invisible world of chemistry and molecules. I completed my schooling at the Convent of Jesus and Mary in Ambala. After that, I did my B.Sc. from Panjab University and my M.Sc. in Chemistry from the well-known Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology. To formally train as a teacher, I also completed my B.Ed. from Kurukshetra University.

Interestingly, my career did not start in a laboratory. I actually started out working as a cabin crew member for a top Indian airline, travelling across the globe. Working in such a demanding and fast-paced environment helped me build strong emotional intelligence and the ability to adapt quickly. Today, these exact skills help me connect deeply with my students and understand them better in an online teaching setup.
