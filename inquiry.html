The Core Practitioner Inquiry

The Driving Dilemma: Nowadays, online education strongly promotes interactive tools as the best way to keep students from losing interest. When I used online quizzes, videos, and virtual labs in my classes, my student's participation definitely went up. But I soon realised an important truth: a student who is just clicking on a screen is not necessarily a thinking student. This study looks at the real difference between a student who is simply playing a digital learning game and a student who is truly understanding the deep and complex concepts of science.

"To what extent do interactive digital tools support authentic conceptual mastery rather than merely promoting an illusion of surface level engagement?"
